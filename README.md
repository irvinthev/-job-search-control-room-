# Job Search Control Room 🗂️

A structured, pipeline-driven job search tracker built in Excel. Designed for senior-level professionals managing multiple active opportunities across referrals, warm intros, and cold applications.

> Manage your search like an operating system — not a panic response.

--llllll

## What's Inside

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **README** | Mission framing, sheet guide, and usage principles |
| **Dashboard** | Live pipeline health — totals, interviews, follow-ups, stale roles, and offers |
| **Tracker** | Full application log with 19 columns covering every stage of the process |
| **Lists** *(hidden)* | Powers all dropdown validations silently |

---

## Tracker Columns

| Column | What It Tracks |
|---|---|
| Company | Hiring organization |
| Role | Job title |
| Status | Current stage (dropdown) |
| Round | Interview round number |
| Priority | High / Medium / Low (dropdown) |
| Target Co. | Is this a dream company? Yes / No |
| Warm Path | Cold apply / Referral / Warm intro / Internal champion |
| Target Comp | Compensation range you're targeting |
| Score | Your 1–10 interest and fit rating |
| Applied | Date application was submitted |
| Last Touch | Date of most recent contact |
| Follow-Up Due | Date your next follow-up is due |
| Days Open | Auto-calculated days since last touch |
| Status Risk | Auto-label: ✓ Current / ⚠ Follow-Up Due / ⚠ Stale |
| Hiring Mgr / Recruiter | Primary contact |
| Referral / Champion | Who is advocating for you internally |
| Why This Role | Your honest pitch to yourself |
| Key Risks | What could block or derail this opportunity |
| Decision Notes | Notes for when comparing final-round offers |

---

## Dashboard KPIs

The Dashboard tab auto-calculates from the Tracker:

- **Total Roles** — all active rows
- **Interviewing** — Recruiter Screen + Interviewing + Final Round
- **Follow-Up Due** — roles past their follow-up date (excluding closed)
- **Stale (>14 days)** — roles with no touch in over 2 weeks
- **High Priority** — roles flagged as High priority
- **Offers** — active offers in hand

---

## Features

- ✅ Dropdown validation for Status, Priority, Warm Path, and Target Co.
- ✅ Status Risk auto-label (formula-driven, no manual updates needed)
- ✅ Conditional formatting — Offers (green), Rejected (red), On Hold (yellow), Stale roles (orange), Overdue follow-ups (yellow)
- ✅ Days Open calculated live from Last Touch date
- ✅ Frozen header row for easy scrolling
- ✅ Excel Table structure for auto-expanding rows and structured formula references

---

## How to Use

1. **Add a row** for every role you apply to, network toward, or actively consider
2. **Update Status** after every call, screen, or interview — same day
3. **Set Follow-Up Due** every time you touch a role
4. **Use Key Risks** to force honest thinking — not every role deserves equal energy
5. **Use Why This Role** to keep your story sharp going into interviews
6. **Check the Dashboard** weekly to see where your pipeline is healthy and where it's stalling

## Notes

- All company names, contacts, and referral names in the example data are **entirely fictional** — created for illustration purposes only
- The tracker is optimized for senior individual contributor and leadership roles (Director, Sr. Manager, GRC, Security, Operations)
- Works best in **Microsoft Excel** — some formula features may behave differently in Google Sheets

## Background

Built after comparing multiple job search trackers and combining the best elements of each into a single, pipeline-driven system. The goal was to stop tracking applications and start tracking **opportunity paths**.
